About Me

Hi there! I'm Jimmy.

Welcome to my homepage. I'm currently employed as a Cloud Data Engineering Intern at Discover Financial Services. More specifically, I've been spending my summer with the Cloud Data Enablement team, an agile software development team that is creating data pipelines from our on-site Teradata servers to the Amazon Web Services cloud.

I'm also a recent graduate of Miami University, a school that I chose for it's powerful academics, the emphasis it places on undergraduate student success, and the dedicated faculty. Needless to say, I loved my college experience and am a proud alumnus.

During my time at Miami, I was able to work as an undergraduate teaching assistant in the department of computer science, study abroad for a semester in Luxembourg, and took part in everything from intramural softball and music ensembles to bartending and volunteering. I chose to major in Mathematics and Statistics because of the immense versatility that a strong foundation in these two fields can provide.

I recently made a bit of a pivot in my career. I spent the summer of 2017 as an Actuarial and Finance intern for Blue Cross Blue Shield Association. It was a wonderful experience, but it helped me to realize that it wasn't quite the career for me. I decided to pursue something that was a bit more technical and set my sights on computer science and software engineering. It included adding a CS minor and significantly more work for my last year of college, but the experience has been incredibly worthwhile. I'm looking forward to a rewarding career in technology.
